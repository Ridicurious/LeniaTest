About NexusAI

Founded in 2018 by a team of AI researchers and scientists from Stanford and MIT, NexusAI was born from the frustration of seeing groundbreaking research buried in silos and the slow pace of scientific discovery.

Our mission is to accelerate human progress by empowering researchers with AI tools that can process, analyze, and connect scientific information at unprecedented scale and speed.

Today, our platform is used by over 5,000 researchers across 40 countries, contributing to advancements in medicine, materials science, energy, and beyond.
